# Anamalia Film Bible v1.0.0

## Global Visual Constants

### Camera System
- Lens: 35mm
- Height: Eye-level, tripod at 1m
- Angle: 5° downward tilt
- Framing: Consistent across all shots

### Lighting Setup
- Primary: Warm late-afternoon sunlight (4800K)
- Direction: Front-left at 45° angle
- Fill: Soft fill from right side
- Rim: Subtle rim light for depth
- Shadows: Diffused floor shadows

### Material DNA
- Body: Felted wool with visible fibers
- Eyes: Hyper-realistic, expressive
- Nose: Detailed, realistic texture
- Gestures: Human-style expressive movements
- Scale: Miniature stop-motion aesthetic

### Backdrop Geometry
- Floor: Matte off-white
- Wall: Matte off-white
- Junction: 90° angle where floor meets wall
- Shadows: Subtle contact shadows

### Color & Tone
- Contrast: Soft, gentle
- Palette: Warm, golden undertones
- Film Look: Ektachrome-style
- Mood: Nostalgic, handcrafted warmth

### Emotional Tone
- Atmosphere: Warm, optimistic, familiar
- Expressions: Humanlike but not exaggerated
- Energy: Gentle humor, nostalgia
- Craft: Handcrafted, artisanal feel

## Continuity Anchors
These elements must remain constant across all renders:
- Camera position and angle
- Lighting direction and temperature
- Material texture and feel
- Backdrop geometry
- Overall emotional tone
